## Workflow

1. First, think about the problem and write a plan in tasks/todo.md.
2. The plan is a checklist of todo items.
3. Check with me before starting to code. I want to verify the plan.
4. Then, complete the todo list one by one, marking them off as you go.
5. At each step, give me a high-level explanation of what you changd.
6. Keep every change simple and minimal. Avoid big rewrites.
7. After completing each todo item, you add a commit following the rule in .github/copilot-instructions.md
8. At the end, add a README.md file to summarize the application.
